Romy Bompart obtained his B.E. degree from Universidad Nacional Experimental Politecnica 'Antonio Jose de Sucre' (UNEXPO), Venezuela, in the year of 2011. Currently, he is pursuing his M.E from Universidad Autonoma de Nuevo Leon, Mexico. He worked as Hardware Design Engineer in The Factory HKA from 2013 to 2014. He joined Yazaki North America in 2014 as Product Engineering Group Lead. His areas of research include power electronics, dc-dc converters and machine learning.
